Welcome to Sushi 2

Experience the finest authentic Japanese cuisine in the heart of San Diego. At Sushi 2, we've been serving delicious sushi and traditional Japanese dishes for nearly two decades. Our commitment to quality ingredients and expert craftsmanship has made us a local favorite.

From fresh sashimi to creative rolls, our talented chefs prepare each dish with care and precision. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch or a relaxing dinner, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.
