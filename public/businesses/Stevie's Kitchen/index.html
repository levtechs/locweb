About Stevie's Kitchen

Welcome to Stevie's Kitchen, Mobile's premier locally-owned deli! We've been serving the community with fresh, quality sandwiches and Southern comfort food.

Our award-winning gumbo is a customer favorite, made fresh daily with authentic Southern recipes. Whether you're craving a hearty sandwich, a fresh salad, or need a quick dinner solution, we've got you covered with our frozen take-home meals.

Enjoy our cozy indoor seating or relax in our pleasant outdoor patio area. We pride ourselves on friendly service, great prices, and food that keeps customers coming back!
