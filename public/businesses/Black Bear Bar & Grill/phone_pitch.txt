Phone Pitch Script - Black Bear Bar & Grill

When calling the business, introduce yourself and your service:

"Hi, I'm calling from [Your Company/Service]. We help local businesses build professional websites to attract more customers.

I noticed Black Bear Bar & Grill doesn't have a website yet. I wanted to reach out because having a professional online presence can really help a bar like yours:

- Customers can easily find your hours, location, and contact info
- You can showcase your great reviews and atmosphere
- People can see your photos and get excited about visiting
- It's free advertising that works 24/7

We build complete, mobile-friendly websites for local businesses at a great price. It's a simple way to get more people through your doors.

Would you be interested in learning more? I can send you some examples of websites we've built for other bars and restaurants."

Key talking points:
- Emphasize the "right to try" - no commitment needed
- Offer to send information first before any decision
- Highlight that it's a local business helping local businesses
- Keep it brief and friendly
