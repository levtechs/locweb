About Tower Cafe

Tower Cafe is your local destination for exceptional breakfast and lunch experiences in Williamsport. Our menu features a delightful variety of dishes from classic Reubens to innovative California Benedicts, all prepared with care and served in an aesthetic dining environment.

We're known for our uniquely delicious baked goods and expertly crafted coffees that keep customers coming back. Whether you're stopping in for a quick coffee or enjoying a leisurely meal with friends, our friendly staff ensures every visit is memorable.
