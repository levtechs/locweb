Welcome to Spice Monkey

Located in one of Oakland's most iconic buildings featuring a stunning mural, Spice Monkey offers a unique dining experience that combines bold Asian flavors with a relaxed bar atmosphere.

Whether you're here for our famous pork belly Asian tacos, crispy cornmeal chicken fingers, or craft IPAs, our menu has something for everyone. Join us for live comedy shows and special events!
