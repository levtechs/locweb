A LA Favorite Since Day One

The Original Pantry Cafe has been a beloved Los Angeles institution for over 20 years. Known locally as "The Original," we're famous for our hearty breakfasts, generous portions, and that classic diner atmosphere that keeps locals coming back night after night.

Whether you're craving fluffy pancakes, mouthwatering ham and sausage, or our legendary hash browns with the perfect crispy exterior, we've got you covered. Don't forget to ask for our famous salsa - customers say it's the best they've ever had!
