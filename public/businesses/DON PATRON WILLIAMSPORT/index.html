Our Story

Welcome to Don Patron

Don Patron brings the authentic taste of Mexico to Williamsport, Pennsylvania. Our passionate chefs prepare traditional Mexican dishes using fresh, quality ingredients and time-honored recipes that have been passed down through generations.

From our famous Chile Rellenos to our mouthwatering birria tacos and delicious burritos loaded with seafood, every dish is crafted with care and served with pride. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, enjoying a romantic date night, or simply craving incredible Mexican cuisine, Don Patron is the perfect destination.
