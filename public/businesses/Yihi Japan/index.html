Welcome to Yihi Japan

Located in the heart of Cleveland at 230 W Huron Rd, Yihi Japan brings you authentic Japanese cuisine with a modern twist. Our expert chefs prepare each dish with care, using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking techniques.

From our famous teriyaki bowls to handcrafted soba noodles, every dish is made to order and served with warm hospitality. Whether you're heading to a ballgame at Progressive Field or exploring downtown Cleveland, we're your go-to destination for delicious Japanese food.
