About Manna Cafe

Located in the corner of the main lobby floor in the Bank of America building on East Cary Street, Manna Cafe has been serving downtown Richmond's workforce and visitors with quality lunches for years.

Despite having no cooking hood and doing everything on hot plates, our sandwiches are simply superb. We're known for our incredible soups, including turkey rice, chicken noodle, chicken pot pie, and our famous spicy turkey chili.

The box lunch is the best deal in Shockoe Bottom - any sandwich, sub, or wrap comes with chips, a cookie, and a soda. Come experience why locals keep coming back!
