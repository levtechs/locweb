Our Story

Galveston Steakhouse has been a Michigan City tradition for over 25 years. Our commitment to quality cuts, exceptional service, and a warm atmosphere keeps families coming back year after year.

Every steak is hand-selected and cooked to perfection. Whether you're craving our famous prime rib on Friday and Saturday nights, a tender ribeye, or one of our many non-beef options, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.
