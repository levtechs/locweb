About Crepes de Luxe

Welcome to Crepes de Luxe, your destination for handcrafted crepes at the historic West Side Market in Cleveland! Since opening our stand, we've been serving up delicious sweet and savory crepes made fresh to order.

Our customers love our signature Montreal-style crepes and Citron Creme - both have become local favorites. Whether you're craving a classic sweet treat or a satisfying savory option, we have something for everyone.

With vegetarian options available and reasonable prices, Crepes de Luxe is perfect for a quick snack or a delicious meal during your market visit.
