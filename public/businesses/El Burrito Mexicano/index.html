Welcome to El Burrito Mexicano

Located at the Nashville Farmers Market, El Burrito Mexicano has been serving the community with authentic, homemade Mexican food made with love and care.

Everything is custom-ordered with tons of options to choose from. Our burritos are famously huge, and our breakfast burritos are the best in Nashville according to our loyal customers.

From beef enchiladas to breakfast burritos loaded with eggs and your choice of toppings, we use fresh ingredients and traditional recipes to bring you the authentic flavors of Mexico.
