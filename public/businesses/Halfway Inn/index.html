About Halfway Inn

Halfway Inn has been serving the Danville community for years, offering a welcoming atmosphere where locals gather for cold drinks, great conversation, and exciting gaming. Whether you're looking for a relaxed evening with friends or some entertainment on our gaming machines, we've got you covered.
