Authentic Turkish & Mediterranean Flavors

Welcome to Gengiz Khan Restaurant, where tradition meets taste in the heart of Tampa. Our restaurant brings you the finest Turkish and Mediterranean cuisine, crafted with authentic recipes and the freshest ingredients.

From perfectly seasoned kebabs to creamy hummus and savory grape leaves, every dish tells a story of rich culinary heritage. We take pride in serving halal-certified meat, ensuring quality and tradition in every bite.

Whether you're joining us for a family dinner, catching up with friends, or hosting a private event, our spacious establishment and warm hospitality await you.
