About Triple Nickel Tavern

Great Drinks, Great Prices

Enjoy cheap draft beers and dive bar styled cocktails at wallet-friendly prices. Try our famous $2.50 Tecate cans or explore our selection of craft beers and spirits.

Legendary Pizza

Our on-site Mojo's Pizza is a local favorite — customers rave about the pizza being "100% masters of their art." Don't leave without trying it!

Authentic Dive Vibes

With punk dive energy and a strong local following, Triple Nickel reminds patrons of favorite NYC haunts. Great music, friendly bartenders, and zero pretensions.

Perfect Location

Located at 103 S Wahsatch Ave #106 in downtown Colorado Springs, we're easily accessible and ready to welcome you seven days a week.
