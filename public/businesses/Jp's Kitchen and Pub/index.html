Welcome to Jp's Kitchen

Welcome to Jp's Kitchen and Pub, your local destination for homemade meals and fresh bakery items in Fountain City, Wisconsin.

Known for our delicious homemade soups, sandwiches, and delectable bakery treats, we take pride in serving quality food with a personal touch. From our famous cookie trays to daily lunch specials, every item is made with care.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick bite, ordering catering for your event, or picking up fresh baked goods, our friendly staff welcomes you like family.
